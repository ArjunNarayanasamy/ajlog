This is the first post. The site is live.

I'm using this as a space to write about tech, ideas, and whatever else is worth putting into words. No particular schedule. No particular audience. Just writing to think.

## what this place is

- **posts** — longer, considered pieces
- **notes** — shorter, rougher thoughts
- **reads** — links I keep coming back to

## why hugo

Static, fast, no database, no CMS, deploys as files. Writing is just Markdown. Fits the way I think about tools: do one thing, do it well, stay out of the way.
