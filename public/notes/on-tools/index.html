on tools

May 22, 2026

A good tool disappears. You stop thinking about the tool and start thinking about the work.

Most software fails this. It makes itself the center of attention — notifications, settings, updates, opinions about your workflow.

The best tools I’ve used have this in common: they do exactly one thing, they do it fast, and they get out of the way.
